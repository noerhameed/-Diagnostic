People Diagnostic Tool

How are you really doing at work?

This diagnostic is designed to help you identify your current engagement state at work and the recommended actions to address it.

Select the response that most closely reflects your experience right now. This is a reflective tool, not a clinical assessment. It is a structured starting point for a conversation.

Any conversation you choose to have with your manager or People Partner following this is confidential.

Time to complete: approximately five minutes.
